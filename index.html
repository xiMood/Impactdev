About the Conference

Where Innovation Meets Clinical Practice

IMPACT is a student-organized conference at Beni Suef National University, designed to foster dialogue between great doctors, emerging researchers, and astonishing medical and pharmacy students.

Attend keynote lectures, panel discussions, and exciting activities spanning the full spectrum of medicine, pharmacy, and clinical therapeutics.

Driving the Future of Healthcare
IMPACT bridges the gap between scientific discovery and patient care, empowering the next generation of healthcare professionals.
